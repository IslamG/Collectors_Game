How to play:

Items will fall gradually and it's your job to catch them! Move your mouse to the right or left to move the basket under the falling items.
When the item collides it will be caught and you will gain 10 points. But be careful! If you miss an item and it drops to the bottom you will lose 10 points.

Be warned!

Don't catch any items in the Avoid section, because these items will subtract 30 points
